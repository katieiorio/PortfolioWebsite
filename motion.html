To sharpen my skills with After Effects and motion graphics, I created this video that encapsulates everything I do as a designer. My main focus was to hone in on my transition skills while also experimenting with graphics and timing.

I first designed all of the visual elements in Illustrator and Photoshop using layers. Then after importing into After Effects, I brought the elements to life using keyframes and effects.
