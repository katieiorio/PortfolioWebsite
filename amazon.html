A+ Content is a powerful advertising tool exclusive to Amazon sellers, enabling them to personalize product pages. Typically designed in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, this digital content elevates the online shopping journey while reinforcing brand identity.

Another form of visual advertising on Amazon is through alternate product images. Sellers are encouraged to boost their products through engaging infographics, size charts or model imagery. This content, designed in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, enhances the user experience through dynamic and engaging artwork. Branding and photography were substituted for confidentiality purposes.
