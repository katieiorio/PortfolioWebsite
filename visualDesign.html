Unwind & Grind is a fictional coffee shop brand for which I designed a variety of branding elements.

I began this project by nailing down a solid aesthetic and brand appearance. I chose soft blues to align with the "unwind" theme of the coffee shop. I used Adobe Illustrator to create two simple, yet effective brand logos that are easily recognizable, even when sized down. Creating mockups which implemented the branding brought the coffee shop to life.

I then designed a mobile app for the business. Online ordering has a large market, especially in the coffee industry. I drew inspiration from coffee applications that I use frequently (maybe even a little too frequently) to help me craft a seamless user experience and user interface. I created the layouts using Figma, then after exporting the artboards, used photoshop to create smartphone mockups.

This project combined my love for coffee with my design skills and I am excited to continue improving this project. I plan to design a website for the brand and eventualy move on to develop both the app and website.
