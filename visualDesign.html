Unwind & Grind

Illustrator

Photoshop

Figma

Unwind & Grind is a fictional coffee shop brand for which I designed a variety of branding elements.

I began this project by nailing down a solid aesthetic and brand appearance. I chose soft blues to align with the "unwind" theme of the coffee shop. I used Adobe Illustrator to create two simple, yet effective brand logos that are easily recognizable, even when sized down. Creating mockups which implemented the branding brought the coffee shop to life.

I then designed a mobile app for the business. Online ordering has a substantial market, especially in the coffee industry. I drew inspiration from coffee applications that I use frequently (maybe even a little too frequently) to help me craft a seamless user experience and user interface. I created the layouts using Figma, then after exporting the artboards, used Adobe Photoshop to create smartphone mockups.

This project combined my love for coffee with my design skills and I am excited to continue improving this project. I plan to design a website for the brand and eventually move on to develop both the app and website.

Brand Board

Illustrator

Photoshop

The brand board embodies the essence of Unwind & Grind. The coffee shop band board is designed with a palette inspired by the quality and simplicity of the coffee. The fonts are carefully selected to blend boldness with approachability, ensuring clear communication of the offerings. Three logos were designed to serve various purposes for the brand: packaging, employee merchandise, and digital usage. Although this brand is fictional, I know I would be inclined to try Unwind & Grind for my next cup of coffee.

Mobile App User Interface

Figma

Illustrator

Photoshop

The Unwind & Grind mobile app customer experience started as wireframes sketched on print out smartphone drawings. I drew inspiration from one of my local coffee shops, Rook who uses an app for mobile orders. When the wireframes were complete, I utilized Figma to build prototypes. This is where the vision begins to come to life. When complete, I exported the artboards and leveraged free iPhone mockups to get the true look and feel of the mobile app on a real phone.

Fashion Collection Look Book Cover Art

Photoshop

While interning at Tia Cibani Kids LLC, I was able to assist with the Fall/Winter 2021 campaign photoshoot. This included catering to the models and organizing the clothing for the shoot. After the shoot was complete, I created a cover for the look book, which showcased the best shots from the photoshoot. I incorporated Tia’s established brand of bright pops of color and playful typefaces. This was a fun project because I was able to see the process from start to finish. This design was created using Adobe Photoshop and later used for the print and digital versions of the look book.

Tia’s Craft Kit Upcycling Project

Photoshop

Illustrator

During my time working with children’s clothing designer Tia Cibani, she asked me to design branded labels and information cards. The print material would be used for Tia’s Craft Kits, small packages that carried unused design materials that would be included in every product order. This project was meant to reuse fashion design materials, while encouraging kids to create their own toys, jewelry, or accessories. I took elements from Tia’s current brand, such as her font and yellow dot pattern for the design. I used Adobe Illustrator to create these designs, then printed them myself in-house at the Tia Cibani studio office.
