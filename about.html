About

Welcome to my custom portfolio website, which I coded from scratch from beginning to end. I hope you take the time to browse a few of my favorite projects which showcase my various design skills.

As a multimedia designer and developer, I create immersive experiences and bring ideas to life. With my creative capabilities spanning across user experience, web development, motion graphics, visual design, and branding, I pride myself on my passionate motivation to explore and build systems that help people. Currently, I specialize in creating advertising content for products sold on Amazon.

With a Bachelor of Arts in Graphic and Interactive Design as well as a Master of Science in Interactive Media and Communications, I have gained a well rounded education and valuable experience in design. I am prepared to make an impact through creativity, skills, and problem solving abilities.

My commitment to a human-centered approach that considers the long term impact is what makes me a powerful creative. With my attention to detail and willingness to learn, I am easily adaptable to different tasks and environments.

I look forward to every step in my evolution as a designer and developer.
